Characters in Noli Me Tangere
Crisostomo
Ibarra
  • He went to Europe for school for 7 years
  • He was set up with Maria Clara by the approval of his father, Rafael Ibbara, and Captain Tiago
  • He is smart, mannered, and kind
  • Image Reference: Wiki, C. T. N. M. T. (n.d.). Crisostomo Ibarra. Noli Me Tangere Wiki. https://noli-me-tangere.fandom.com/wiki/Crisostomo_Ibarra
Maria Clara
  • She is the daughter of Dona Pia Alba who died giving birth to her
  • She had a caretaker, Tiya Isabel, to help her when she was a childr
  • She was studied in Sta. Clara when Ibarra went to Europe for 7 years.
  • Image Reference: Wiki, C. T. N. M. T. (n.d.-b). Maria Clara. Noli Me Tangere Wiki. https://noli-me-tangere.fandom.com/wiki/Maria_Clara
Kapitan Tiago
  • He is a rich businessman in San Diego
  • He was a close friend of Don Rafael Ibarra, Ibarra's Dad
  • While Don Rafael was alive, Kapitan Tiago and him betrothed their childen to one another
  • Image Reference: Wiki, C. T. N. M. T. (n.d.-a). Capitan Tiago. Noli Me Tangere Wiki. https://noli-me-tangere.fandom.com/wiki/Capitan_Tiago
Tiya Isabel
  • She is the one of the caretakers of Maria Clara
  • She and Kapitan Tiago took her in even when she wasn't their legitamate child
  • She became the sole mother figure throughout Maria Clara's childhood.

Don Rafael Ibarra
  • He is the father of Ibarra and was quite generous
  • Before he died, he was the richest man in San Diego
  • He was an enemy of the high elites, and eventually died from them.
  • Image Reference: Personality List. (n.d.). Don Rafael Ibarra Personality type, MBTI - Which personality? https://personalitylist.com/profile/455013/noli-me-tangere-el-filibusterismo/don-rafael-ibarra/
Padre Salvi
  • He was a sickly young curate of San Diego
  • He is not a friend of Ibarra, but dislikes him
  • He is also manipulative and controlling using his postion in church to do such things
  • Image Reference: Wiki, C. T. N. M. T. (n.d.-d). Padre Salvi. Noli Me Tangere Wiki. https://noli-me-tangere.fandom.com/wiki/Padre_Salvi
Padre Damaso
  • He is an arrogant and mean friar of the church
  • He opposed the marriage between Ibarra and Maria Clara
  • He even insulted the people of the Philippines, even though he is one himself
  • Image Reference: Wiki, C. T. N. M. T. (n.d.-d). Padre Damaso. Noli Me Tangere Wiki. https://noli-me-tangere.fandom.com/wiki/Padre_Damaso
