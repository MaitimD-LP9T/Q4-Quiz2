The Philippines is one of the many places that can easily surprise many toursits who visit this country. From the vast mountains and countryside to the cool and hot areas o our beaches. Regardless, the Philippines is a place you can visit today to see all we can give. In fact, here are a couple places most would visit
There are 3 places in particular

Cebu, Kawasan Falls

If you like jumping off waterfalls, Kawasan Falls in Cebu hits the sweet spot. The first cascade is where most visitors stay, swim, and enjoy a natural water massage on board a bamboo raft.

Boracy, White Beach

It is one of the places that can offer an Island Hopping Adventure which attracts tourists. Apart from that it also provides excellent hospitality with its hotels and fresh seafood.

Palawan, Tubbataha Reef

Tubbataha Reef is regarded as one of the top diving spots in the Philippines. Located at the heart of the Sulu Sea, it is considered a center of marine biodiversity. The only way to get to this marine sanctuary is through a liveaboard vessel from Puerto Princesa, Palawan.
